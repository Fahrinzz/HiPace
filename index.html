What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our minimum order quantity is 25 pieces per design. This applies to all product types including jerseys, training wear, and corporate sportswear. The 25 MOQ ensures we can allocate the right production resources while keeping per-unit costs competitive for our B2B clients.
What file formats do you accept for designs?
We accept AI (Adobe Illustrator), EPS, PDF, and high-resolution PNG files (minimum 300 DPI). Vector formats (AI/EPS) are preferred for the sharpest print quality. If you only have a low-resolution logo, our free design consultation team can assist with vectorization.
How long does production and delivery take?
Standard production time is 7–14 working days after mockup approval. Rush orders (subject to availability) can be completed in 5–7 working days. Delivery within Peninsular Malaysia takes 1–3 working days after production. East Malaysia is 3–5 working days.
Do you offer samples before bulk ordering?
Yes. We offer physical fabric swatches and sample jerseys for quality assessment before committing to a bulk order. Sample production is chargeable but the cost is deductible from your final bulk order invoice. Contact our sales team to arrange a sample.
What printing methods do you use?
We offer three main printing methods: Sublimation (all-over print, ideal for jerseys), Heat Transfer (for precision logo placement), and Embroidery (for premium corporate wear). Our team will recommend the most suitable method based on your design, fabric type, and quantity.
Can I get a custom colour for my garment?
Absolutely. We support any Pantone colour code for custom fabric dyeing and sublimation printing. Simply provide your brand colour hex code or Pantone reference during consultation and our team will match it precisely to your requirements.
